Cinque Terre

Cinque Terre is a collection of five seaside villages on the Ligurian Coast of Italy. The villages are famous for their colorful houses and beautiful scenery.

Santorini

Santorini is a volcanic island in the Cyclades group of the Greek Islands. The island is famous for its picturesque villages, stunning sunsets, and beautiful beaches.

Machu Picchu

Machu Picchu is an ancient Incan city in the Andes Mountains of Peru. The city was built in the 15th century and is considered one of the most important archaeological sites in the world.
